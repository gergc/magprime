Where do I go when I arrive? How do I pick up my badge?
When you arrive at MAGFest, go to Registration, located in Expo Hall E, to pick up your badge. Once Registration opens on Thursday morning, usually at 10:00 AM, it will stay open until Sunday afternoon at 1:00 PM, with three exceptions. Registration will close between 4:00AM and 7:00AM Friday, Saturday, and Sunday mornings. Use our map in Guidebook to help find your way to Registration on the Expo level of the convention center. Please bring a government-issued photo id such as a driver's license or military id.

Will additional badges be sold at the door?
Yes, but there is a possibility of us selling out, so check our website for any ticket availability alerts.

Which registration line do I use?

  • Volunteers, Staff, Contractors, Bands, Guests, Vendors, Indie showcase exhibitors, and Panelists share the same line. Look for the signage inside Expo Hall E.
  • All other pre-registered attendees (including those who purchased group registrations) should use the "Pre-Registration Badge Pickup" line inside Expo Hall E.
  • If you are accompanied by people who have not pre-registered, they can purchase their badge online. Cash and Credit are accepted. Once registered, proceed to the At-Door badge pickup line to make any needed cash payments, or head to one of the PreRegistration lines.

Where can I pick up merch that I pre-ordered, such as a merch package?
After you've picked up your badge, you can pick up your merch at the pre-order merch pickup station at the front of Expo E (Thursday & Friday) or the at the MAGFest merch booth (Saturday & Sunday). The merch booth is located at the back of Expo Hall C. Use our map in Guidebook to help find your way to the merch booth from the registration area.

Where can I view a schedule of events?
There are multiple ways to view our schedule:

  • Using the Guidebook app or website (desktop or mobile): Go to the Guidebook website to view the guidebook in your web browser or download the mobile app. Make sure to check for Guidebook updates periodically through the weekend, as we may make schedule changes.
  • Paper schedules: Printed schedules will be available in limited quantities at Registration and our Info Desk. Please consider using Guidebook instead to save paper.
